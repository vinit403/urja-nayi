✦ MY STORY

Born to lead, built to explore.

I’m Urja Nayi — born on 8th October 2001 in Surat, Gujarat. The name Urja means energy, and that’s exactly what drives everything I do. A science student at heart, I pursued my BSc in Chemistry from Sheth P.T. Mahila College, Surat.

While still building my education, I stepped into the professional world at just 19 — joining as a Customer Care Executive in 2020. Every call, every interaction, and every challenge taught me how to truly understand and serve people.

Those years became the foundation of Ananta Urja Travels — built with the belief that every person deserves a journey designed uniquely for them.
